Типы заданий:

Multiple choice - выбор правильного ответа
True/False/Not Given - определение истинности утверждений
Yes/No/Not Given - согласие с мнением автора
Matching headings - подбор заголовков к параграфам
Sentence completion - завершение предложений
Summary completion - заполнение пропусков в резюме
Short answer questions - краткие ответы на вопросы
Matching information - соотнесение информации


Вот примеры каждого типа вопросов в IELTS Reading:

1. Multiple Choice
Вопрос: What is the main cause of global warming according to the passage?
A) Solar radiation
B) Human activities
C) Natural cycles
D) Ocean currents


2. True/False/Not Given
Утверждения:

The research was conducted over a 10-year period. (True/False/Not Given)
Scientists used advanced equipment in their study. (True/False/Not Given)
The results surprised the research team. (True/False/Not Given)


3. Yes/No/Not Given
Вопрос: Do the following statements agree with the writer's opinion?

Technology will solve all environmental problems. (Yes/No/Not Given)
Governments should invest more in renewable energy. (Yes/No/Not Given)


4. Matching Headings
Заголовки:
i) Economic benefits of tourism
ii) Environmental concerns
iii) Future predictions
iv) Historical background
Параграфы: A, B, C, D (нужно сопоставить с заголовками)


5. Sentence Completion
Завершите предложения словами из текста (не более 3 слов):

The experiment lasted for __________.
Participants were selected based on their __________.
The main finding was that __________ significantly improved.


6. Summary Completion
Заполните пропуски:
The study examined the effects of (1)________ on plant growth. Researchers found that plants exposed to (2)________ grew 25% faster than the control group. The results suggest that (3)________ could revolutionize agriculture.


7. Short Answer Questions
Ответьте не более чем тремя словами:

When did the Industrial Revolution begin?
Where was the first factory built?
Who invented the steam engine?


8. Matching Information
Сопоставьте информацию с параграфами A-F:

A description of the research methodology (Paragraph ___)
Statistics about population growth (Paragraph ___)
An example of successful implementation (Paragraph ___)

Каждый тип требует разных стратегий чтения и поиска информации в тексте.
